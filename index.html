Copper Oxidizer

Have you ever hated how you can revert back the copper oxidation state, but not control it at your will? Now you can! Just use a water bucket in any copper block (slab and stair included) and the will oxidize a bit each time! If you want to use a water bucket as a regular item, just shift right-click!
